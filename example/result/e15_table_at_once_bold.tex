\chapter{Table in one call with bold first row example}

This is a table with bold text in first row.

\noindent
\begin{tabular}{lll}
\toprule
\textbf{Name} & \textbf{Age} & \textbf{City} \\
\midrule
Janet & 25 & New York \\
Jacob & 30 & Los Angeles \\
Jill & 35 & Chicago \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\par\medskip

\end{tabular}
